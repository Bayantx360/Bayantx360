Bayantx360 is a modular AI and analytics platform designed to give researchers, data analysts, students, and enterprise teams access to advanced quantitative tools — without requiring deep programming expertise. Each product in the ecosystem targets a specific workflow, from panel regression to synthetic data generation.
No. All core products are built on a low-code and no-code philosophy. You interact through guided interfaces, upload your data, configure parameters visually, and receive formatted outputs — all without writing a single line of code. PanelStatX is an AI-powered panel regression SaaS. It simplifies econometric modelling — including OLS, Fixed Effects (FE), and Random Effects (RE) estimation — and generates automated, publication-ready regression tables and diagnostic reports. It's ideal for academic researchers, economists, and policy analysts working with panel datasets.
DataSynthX generates synthetic datasets that statistically mirror your original data. It's designed for researchers who need privacy-safe alternatives to sensitive datasets, developers who need test data, and teams who need to augment limited datasets for model training. The synthetic output preserves distributional properties and correlations found in the source data.

Model Specification

The Fixed Effects (Two-Way) estimator is appropriate — the Hausman test (χ²=14.83, p=0.002) strongly rejects Random Effects, confirming entity effects are correlated with regressors.

Key Findings

  • –NPM is the sole significant predictor (β=0.0326***, t=12.19) — a 1-unit rise in Net Profit Margin increases ROA by 3.26 pp.
  • –ROCE and DPS are statistically insignificant at the 5% level.
  • –JB test (p<0.001) rejects normality; HAC-robust standard errors recommended.
